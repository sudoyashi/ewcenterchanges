Learning to Speak Thai

  • How to Introduce Yourself in Thai (video, 3:00)
  • Thai Greeting and How to WAI (video, 3:00)
  • Learn 25 Must-Know Thai Phrases (video, 7:19)
  • Complete Introduction to Thai in 26 Minutes (video, 26:00)
    You may wish to START with this video if you are serious about learning Thai. You will be introduced to Thai pronunciation, grammar, and writing, and you will see Thai words written in both Thai characters and the alphabet, giving you the tools needed to work toward mastery. Whether picking up basic Thai or pursuing mastery, ThaiPod101.com is one of the best (free) online Thai language learning websites.
